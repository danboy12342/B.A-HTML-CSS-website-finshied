  • In S4, the experience of S1 to S3 is used as the foundation for learning about software development and information systems.
  • In S4, completion of practical outcomes and coursework tasks demonstrates capability at National 4 or National 5 level.
  • The final exam assesses the ability of pupils to discuss and write code as well as explaining technology.

  • In S5, Higher Computing takes the knowledge of Computing gained in S4 to the next level.
  • Introducing complex programming tasks, modelling of data flow inside the computer and explanations of networking and computer hardware.
  • Pupils complete a coursework task that demonstrates their ability in programming and problem solving.

  • Advanced Higher Computing takes the concepts of Higher forward, focusing on algorithms, programming methodology and detailed investigation of Computer Architecture.
  • A project takes up 40% of the grade and is open for pupils to investigate any software development project they wish to carry out.
